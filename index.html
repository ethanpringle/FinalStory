🎮 Choose Your Own Adventure: The Ethics of Copyright

You're a budding creator who just finished a short film project for a school assignment. Now you need to add music to it. What will you do?

  • 👉 Use a popular copyrighted song without permission
  • 👉 Search for royalty-free or Creative Commons music

🎵 Popular Song Without Permission

You add your favorite pop song to the film—it really slaps! You upload the video online.

🚨 Uh-oh! The platform takes it down for copyright infringement, and you get a warning.

Do you...

  • 👉 Try to dispute the copyright claim dishonestly?
  • 👉 Decide to replace the music with a legal option?

🔍 Search for Royalty-Free Music

You find a cool track labeled "CC-BY" on a sharing site. You include it and give credit in your video description.

🎉 Your video stays online, and people love it!

You respected the artist's rights and still made something great.

✅ The End. Ethical Win!

🚫 Dishonest Dispute

You claim the music is yours or that it's "fair use" without a strong case.

❌ Your dispute is rejected. Your account gets restricted.

💡 Lesson: Integrity matters. Respecting creators means not taking what's not yours.

❌ The End. Ethical Fail

🔁 Play again?

🔄 Replace with Legal Music

You swap in a free track with proper attribution. It’s not as trendy, but the video still works.

🎬 Your project gets a passing grade and no copyright issues!

✅ The End. Ethical Win!
